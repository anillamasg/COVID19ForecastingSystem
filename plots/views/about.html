A team of postgraduate students, Anil Lama, Chirayu Gautam, and Srijana Ghimire have developed a COVID-19 Forecasting System as a university project.

CFS features with a visual presentation of COVID-19 cases of the past and prediction for the next 10 days.

CFS assists for deep analysis providing the comparison tool for comparing COVID-19 cases of different locations.

Current version of CFS is featuring with data from Australia and its states only.

A project by AnilL, ChirayuG, and SrijanaG; students of Victoria University.
